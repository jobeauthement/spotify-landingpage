What is Spotify and Spotify Premium?

Spotify is an audio streaming service with over 80 million tracks, podcasts, and audio books.

With Premium, listen to music ad-free and with unlimited skips, at the highest quality, and download anything to listen offline. All for one low price.

How do I cancel?

You can easily cancel your Premium subscription on the Account overview page. You can still listen until the next billing date.

How does the trial work?

If you’ve never had Premium or been a member of a Premium plan such as Family or Duo, try any plan for a reduced rate or even free.

For free trials, you still need to enter a valid payment method to sign up. This is so we can confirm your country or region and process payments if you want to keep Premium after the trial ends.

How does the Duo and Family plan work?

Premium Duo is a discount plan for 2 people who live together.

Premium Family is a discount plan for up to 6 family members who live together, and comes with Spotify Kids and an explicit music filter.

Each member of these plans has their own Premium account. No member needs to share their password, and all saved music and playlists are their own.

How does the student plan work?

Students get discoutted Premium for up to 4 years.

Available to higher education students at supported colleges/universities, verified by SheerID.

To verify, you either log into your college/uni's portal or upload proof of your enrollment.

You don't need to register your student email address to your Spotify account.
